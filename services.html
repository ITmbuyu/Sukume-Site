Building unique African global brands

We design and develop branding, marketing and communication strategies specifically towards growing Africa’s own brands. Through designing strong home-grown brand identities, building their awareness and positive reputations, we help create ample demand for a new generation of globally competitive and locally compelling African consumer, business and economic sector brands.

We have solid experience managing, building and evaluating the economic reputations of African nation brands. The main aim of most of our nation branding efforts is for our client countries to attract their fair share of global trade, foreign direct investment (FDI) and thereafter, tourists and global events.

Boosting genuine inter-Africa regional trade

We facilitate trade & investment with a focus on Inter-Africa Regional Trade, Integration and Economic Indigenisation. By creating demand for “Made by Africa” goods through promoting the “Buy African” concept to African consumers and resellers, we help get Africans to do business with each other. This boosts local manufacturing (industrialisation) while building a strong equity for “Brand Africa” on the Continent and abroad.

We also conduct investment and trade missions taking African business people to explore opportunities in other African and global markets.
