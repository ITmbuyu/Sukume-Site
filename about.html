About Us

Promoting
Transformative Economic Development
in Africa

Sukume is an economic development consultancy with 10 years’ experience focusing on SME Development, Intra-African Trade and Place/Country Branding. We also do Public Speaking on our 3 focus areas. We do this through:

  1. a. Designing and implementing relevant strategies and projects that deliver global competitiveness and socio-economic transformation across African communities
  2. b. Creating and supporting platforms that promote Made-by-African goods and services, particularly for SMEs
  3. c. Facilitating trade and investment between African SMEs and large pan-African corporations, governments, and their global counterparts in ways that foster shared economic growth and empowerment for local communities

Our customers include African economic development entities, places/countries, businesses, and brands.

Despite the significant growth of African economies in the last few decades, this has not translated into much developmental change nor benefit for local people. They remain mired in the triple evils of unemployment, poverty, and inequality. To deal with this, just about every government in Africa recognizes the need for rapid industrialization through local manufacturing and export-led economies.Sukume Consulting is the only economic development consultancy that uses UBUNTU/ BOTHO as a foundation for development planning, implementation, and brand positioning. We designed the Competitiveness & Transformation Planner™ (CTP) model, an IDENTITY-based approach as a tool specifically for building world-class African economies, businesses, and brands in ways that guarantee social and economic transformation among local communities.
