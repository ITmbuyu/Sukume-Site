Transformative Economic Development

Purpose

For the past 10 years and onwards, our purpose is to develop African brands, businesses and economies for global competitiveness and socio-economic transformation. We take our mission so seriously that we work exclusively with clients and partners with the same organisation mandate or project goals of transformative economic development


We believe in co-building with and sharing prosperity with the communities in which we operate. We galvanise and support our clients and partners to do the same.

Capabilities

1. Designing and implementing relevant strategies and projects that deliver global competitiveness and socio-economic transformation across African communities


2. Creating and supporting platforms that promote Made-by-African goods and services, particularly for SMEs


3. Facilitating trade and investment between African SMEs and large pan-African corporations, governments, and their global counterparts in ways that foster shared economic growth and empowerment for local communities

Vision

To see prosperous African economies and businesses rooted in their unique purpose

Mission

To develop African brands, businesses and economies for global competitiveness and socio-economic transformation

Values

Excellence | Integrity | Relationships | Leadership | Authenticity

Vuyelwa Nyakaza

MANAGING DIRECTOR
Entrepreneur | Strategist | Made-by-African Advocate
Professional Background

Founder and Managing Director of Sukume Consulting, Vuyelwa Nyakaza works in the Economic Development space across Africa. With significant experience in Place/Nation Branding, SME Development and Intra-African Trade, she advises high-ranking leaders and organisations in the private, public and non-profit sectors. Vuyelwa is passionate about developing African brands, businesses and economies for global competitiveness and socio-economic transformation.

As a thought leader and advocate of the Buy African concept, Vuyelwa is in the process of creating platforms to promote Made-by-African brands through building awareness, facilitating and providing market acces for them.

As the Global Brand Strategist for the MTN Group, she worked across 22 countries in Africa and the Middle East. Vuyelwa built robust classical marketing experience managing Unilever brands in local (South Africa), regional (Africa & Middle East, Turkey) as well as international (Brazil) roles.


Education

  1. Doctor of Philosophy (candidate) University of South Africa, South Africa
  2. Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing Cape Peninsula University of Technology, South Africa
  3. Master of Arts in Social Anthropology University of London UK

Molapokatse Fanana

CHIEF BRAND MARKETING
Entrepreneur | Creative Director | Brand Strategist
Professional Background

Molapokatse Fanana consults in brand strategy and creative on areas pertaining to the economy, nation and SME development. He is passionate about “Proudly African” concepts for which he advocates. With a successful background as a dynamic businessman and intrepid entrepreneur, Fanana brings with him vast business and branding experience spanning over 15 years. With an uncanny knack for new ideas and a pioneering spirit, Fanana is a founding member of two successful companies: Moving Ads South Africa and Channel Communications Lesotho.

Before the advent of Moving Ads and ChannelCom, Fanana was a marketing professional in SA where he plied his trade for reputable international advertising agency networks like Ogilvy and BBDO. His portfolio includes award-winning work for blue chip brands like Levis, Virgin Atlantic, KFC and DSTv. His work as a communication specialist, brand strategist and a seasoned copy writer garnered recognition that earned him a British D&AD for writing, Cannes Award and New York One Show.

Fanana is currently executing capital projects in areas of Management Consulting including Training Consulting, Capacity Building & Project Management.


Education

  • Master of Business Administration University of Reading, UK
  • Mandela Washington 2015 Fellow Clark-Atlanta University USA

Tuduetso Mokgosi

Tuduetso Mokgosi consults in market research, strategy and creative direction for brand development. Passionate about culture and people insights, her work is rooted in cultural relevance and social impact.
